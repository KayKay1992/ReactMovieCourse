Key Notes:
The navigate('/') function will take users back to your root route

The button has a hover effect using your existing --secondary-color variable

z-index: 10 ensures it stays above the backdrop image

The arrow (←) makes it clear it's a back navigation action

You can customize the button text ("Back to Movies", "← Home", etc.) and styling to match your app's design
